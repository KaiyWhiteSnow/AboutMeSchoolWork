Philosophy

Philosophy is by many considered boring. But is it really? I have
spent hundreds of hours analizing different philosophies. Most memorable
The art of war, The art of not giving a fuck, Taoism but many others
as well. Philosophy became a big part of my life. The biggest influence
being "What does it mean to be a man?" which is a philosophy about
toxic masculinity and how it affects the life of people around us.
"There is no glory in blood, no respect in enemy submission, no honor
in violence, no power in anger when it cannot heal what is lost, only
a fragile hope that these little soliders might come marching home"
-Hello future me, What does it mean to be a man?
