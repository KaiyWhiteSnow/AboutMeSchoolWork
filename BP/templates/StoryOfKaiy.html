My story

The kid who would later be known as Kaiy, was once just an average kid, just making his first nick name to use
for the whole new world called "The internet". I wanted something close to my real name and something that sounds
cool. I decided for the name "JardexX" as the combination of my real name JÃ¡ra and game portal "FortexX Gaming".

This was the begining, a kid, a dream and a whole lot of mistakes to be made. JardexX is to this day the nemesis
of myself. It's a person that I am but no longer want to be. I created new nick name as an escape from my problems.
It was the name my older brother used on one of his accounts in Metin2 - WhiteSnow. It was the image of myself that
I wanted to be. Me who is not a cheater or untrust-worthy liar. However it wasn't long before I realised that I
cannot escape my past.

The nick Kaiy

So this was it. I have experienced my highs and lows and I was ready to face the reality. I can't escape what
I was and the last thing that remains is to find the middle ground. I was not perfect but I have learned.
So after nearly a year, I changed my nick name one last time. This time forever. I chose Kaiy as the
combination of Kay from my favourite show when I was a kid and "iY" to still keep myself reminded of
where I come from. Since then I learned to accept the reality of things and I managed to face myself,
regardless of what life threw at me.

My profile picture actualy captures this progression of my life on the internet. A figure standing
on top of a hill, over looking the landscape. A tree Picea glauca "Sanders Blue" representing my
birth tree, giant cloud representing how great the world really is and most importantly the sun set.
The sun set represents the days which are now long gone. Quote "Even the sun sets alone" has much more
to uncover than what it may seem.
