In detail

Hello, my name is Jára "Kaiy" Judl and I would like to introduce myself to you. I was born on the 28th of Octomber 2006
however my story will start just a bit later. When I was around 6 years old to be exact.

My first computer

When I was just 6 years old I used to watch my brother play games on his computer. He only played 3 games and that being
World of Tanks, Shakes and Fidget and to this day one of my personal favourites Metin2. I used to play on his accounts when
I was young but soon I made my own. Then, just few months later, I instead started using my sisters laptop because she barely
ever used it. This was also the first time I ever broke a computer (I thought I could unfreeze a computer by knocking on its screen).

Why IT?

Before I wanted to be an IT specialist, I wanted to be about as everything every boy has ever wanted. Garbage man, Sniper in the army,
Firefighter, just to name a few. However, I truly realised how much I love technology at just 9 years old when I made my first ever script
that shut down a computer. If I remember correctly it was someting like "Shutdown -s -t 1".

Whats next?

I plan to become a Game Dev. My goal would be game studios like Riot Games, Battlestate Games or Facepunch studios.
